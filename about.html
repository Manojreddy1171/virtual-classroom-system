About Our Institution

Established in 2005, Prathibha School of Engineering has been at the forefront of technical education in the region. Located in Kurnavalli, Khammam District, we have consistently produced engineers who excel in both technical expertise and ethical leadership.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that combines rigorous academics with practical experience, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier engineering institution that develops technically competent, socially responsible professionals capable of addressing global challenges.
